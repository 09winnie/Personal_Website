About

Grace Kendi is one of the most successful, female software engineers in Kenya.She is a tech enthusiast,a passionate radio presenter,a podcaster and a blogger. She is founder of Candy Talks,a website where youths and teens meet and share their experiences about life,family,relationships,careers and mental health.She is mainly interested in mental health as she believes that youths are largely affected by stress and depression and they need people to open up to and be listened to.She advices the youth to believe in their abilities and go for what they believe in.Grace Kendi defines herself as an extroverted introvert,a lover of life and a "shoulder to lean on".Kendi is a mother to a beautiful intelligent little princess. She describes her daughter as her inspiration and the reason why she works so hard,to give her daughter the life she never had.
